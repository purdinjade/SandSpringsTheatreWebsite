About Our Theatre

The Sand Springs Community Theatre is a volunteer-driven, non-profit organization built by and for the residents of Sand Springs. From our actors on stage to our set builders, light technicians, and ushering staff, we thrive on the creative energy of our local community. Get involved! We present a variety of shows (usually four productions a season), drama, comedy, musicals, and original works.

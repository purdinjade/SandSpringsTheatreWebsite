By Sean Grennan

Show Dates:

Synopsis:

The Tin Woman tells the story of Joy, a young woman, who has been given another chance of life when she receives a much-needed heart transplant. She grapples with the feelings of why she should be alive, when someone else had to die for her to get this chance. Why would her life matter? She reaches out to the donor’s family to learn more about the person, whose heart now beats in her chest.
